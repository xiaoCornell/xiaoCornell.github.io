Xiao Wang (王骁)

I am a postdoctoral associate in the Department of Physics at Cornell University, collaborating with Prof. Eun-Ah Kim. I obtained my Ph.D. from the Tsung-Dao Lee Institute at Shanghai Jiao Tong University as a first-batch T. D. Lee doctor.

I mainly work on condensed matter physics, statistical physics and quantum information, specifically looking into conformal field theory, integrability, and their practical applications. I also develop numerical methods like tensor networks, Hamiltonian truncation method and variational quantum algorithms to study quantum many-body physics.
